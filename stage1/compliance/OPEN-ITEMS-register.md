## Standing guardrails (not a task — do not regress)

- **No US vendor** for children's data. `ALLOWED_REGIONS` must only ever be expanded
  with UK/EU values (`stage1_config.py` l.28; `backend/README.md`). Any US candidate →
  RISK + `TODO-FOUNDER` replacement.
- **Non-diagnostic language** is enforced by the i18n lint; do not weaken (keeps the
  product outside MDR/SaMD).
- **No-retention** of video is structural; any exception (e.g. safeguarding, B12) must
  be a deliberate, lawyer-approved decision.
