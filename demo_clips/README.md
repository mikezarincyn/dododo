# Demo clips — **sample clips required**

> **DEMO — sample data only, not for real children's videos.**

This folder is intentionally **empty of media**. The demo must run on **synthetic or
consented ADULT** footage only — **never** real children's videos.

## What to put here

Add 2–3 short clips (a few seconds, `.mp4`/`.mov`) that are either:

- **synthetic** (generated/stock footage of an adult), or
- footage of an **adult who has given explicit consent** to appear in a demo.

Suggested names: `demo_adult_1.mp4`, `demo_adult_2.mp4`.

## How they are used

The app does **not** auto-load these. During a demo, upload a clip from this folder
through the app's normal **video uploader**. Keeping the clips in the repo just makes
them easy to find when presenting.

## Rules (do not break)

- ❌ No real children's videos — ever — in this repo or the deployed demo.
- ❌ Do not commit footage without documented consent.
- ✅ Adult/synthetic + consent only.

If you have no suitable clips yet, leave this folder as-is and the demo simply has no
bundled samples until you add them.
